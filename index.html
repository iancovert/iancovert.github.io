Ian C. Covert

I'm a Ph.D. student in the Paul G. Allen School of Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Washington, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Su-In Lee. I also collaborate with Scott Lundberg from Microsoft Research and Uygar Sümbül from the Allen Institute for Brain Science.

Previously, I worked with Emily Fox at UW and was a student researcher at Google Brain working with Jiening Zhan and Ming Jack Po. Before that, I completed my Bachelor's program at Columbia University, where I did computational neuroscience research in Liam Paninski's group.
